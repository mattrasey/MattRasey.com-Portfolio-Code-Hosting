These bedroom renders were done for my Lighting & Rendering class my junior year. The models were provided and I had to texture everything and light the scene, and worked with the render settings a lot to get the best end result. While one of the easiest parts, my favorite texturing was picking out posters, and ultimately I realized I prefer creating my own models and constructing a scene and texturing it, over just texturing and lighting a scene given to me.
