High 5 Casino Games

In the summer of 2018 I interned at High 5 Games in One World Trade in New York City. I got to learn the ins and outs of casino games, specifically with slot machines. My time there had me designing game assets for 4 different slots in their library of over 200 games. I used Maya, Cinema 4D, and After Effects with Red Giant/Sapphire plug-ins to create static high-pay symbols and 3-5 second winning animations,
