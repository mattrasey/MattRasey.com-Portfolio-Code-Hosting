This frog has protected this pond for years, and with nuclear waste introduced into it, he has mutated to grow horns and a second pair of eyes. This project was done in 2 weeks for a class focused on modeling, lighting, and rendering, and was constructed in 3D Coat and Cinema 4D. Covering the big set of eyes will make the frog look more like a bullfrog, before his genetic mutation developed.
