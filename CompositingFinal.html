My Compositing class final had to have a character enter a portal to another space. As a 3D enthusiast, I decided to construct an animated scene of retro video game characters in Cinema 4D, which has a great camera tracking system built in. The door and outside surroundings that disappear into live footage had a green material applied to them so Keylight in After Effects could green screen them out, leaving just the video game assets to be seen in front of real footage.
