Unscheduled Landing

I designed this alien for my 3D Modeling class sophomore year and went on to model him soon after. This project helped me hone my procedural texturing skills as well as character modeling. Although he may look like a simpler model, I have spent a lot of time perfecting him to my initial design and trying new things out. I went back my senior year and modeled a background and scene to practice grass and fog, and had fun setting up the alien getting pulled over after having too much alien beer and crashing his spaceship in a field. It was once again revisited in 2026 to convert it from Cinema 4D's standard renderer to Redshift to further the lighting, materials, and final look while staying true to the original design from years ago.
