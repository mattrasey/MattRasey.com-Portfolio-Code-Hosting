This was my final for my Modeling, Lighting, and Rendering II class, where I created two different "worlds" that share the same landscapes but differ extremely in assets. The point was to contrast the natural world with little human intervention, with a factory-esque, unsaturated land with fossil fuel energy. The final product was 2 rendered pictures, but I made a quick animation to combine both creations in one solid look.
