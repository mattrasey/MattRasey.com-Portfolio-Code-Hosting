NBC Specialty Extended Graphics

Sunday Night Football, NASCAR, PGA Tour, NHL

Specialty graphics, also known as "vizs" are custom 15-30 second animations showing specific infographics and stats that production deem necessary to showcase during a game. The typical build time is 2 days and here are some still examples of mine that made air.


Football Graphics

Sunday Night Football and Football Night in America Graphics


Different sales graphics, interstitials, transitions, and insert elements made for NBC's football properties. The telestrator is my proudest work, an arduous process of designing and animating 30+ 3pt-loop pieces and versioned out for every team throughout the 2021-2022 season for Super Bowl 56 and used frequently the following SNF seasons.
