I redesigned several NHL logos in InDesign for my Visible Language class. I looked into each team's history and their respective cities to create new and significant logos that I believed better portrayed their brand. In order from left to right, the teams are the Colombus Blue Jackets, Arizona Coyotes, Winnipeg Jets, Edmonton Oilers, Buffalo Sabres, Dallas Stars, Minnesota Wild, Carolina Hurricanes, St. Louis Blues, Tampa Bay Lightning, and the Colorado Avalanche. Some design features to look for are the initials in the Coyote's ear, the Canadian maple leaf in the Jet, and the Hurricane Warning Flags in the Hurricane's logo.
