Video Game Portal

This assembly line/Rube Goldberg machine was a personal project completed in the summer of 2017 to have fun animating, modeling, lighting, and rendering in a different style then what I am used to. I relied heavily on luminance and glow for materials, and did plenty of experimenting with metallic reflectances. This project was spread out over 2 weeks and each frame rendered in about 5 minutes, mainly because of high anti-aliasing settings and the material's specularity.
